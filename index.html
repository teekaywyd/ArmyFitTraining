My Story

FROM THE GYM FLOOR TO YOUR GOALS.

I've spent over 8 years working with everyone from first-time gym-goers to competitive athletes. What I've learned is this: the body is always capable of more than the mind believes — and my job is to bridge that gap.

I started training because I needed it myself. I was overweight, underconfident, and frustrated with every programme that promised the world and delivered nothing. So I studied obsessively, got certified, and built a method that actually works — one that respects your individuality while refusing to accept your excuses.

Every client who walks through the door gets the same treatment: total commitment, radical honesty, and a plan that fits their real life. That's the ArmyFit standard.
